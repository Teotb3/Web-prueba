About Me

I compose, produce and design sound for film, games and audiovisual storytelling.

I am Teo Torres Basulto, a composer and producer focused on music for film, video games and audiovisual media.

Music is a fundamental part of audiovisual work, but it is often pushed aside or left until the last moment. By working with me, you can think about music from the beginning of the project and reach a more complete result, with music that truly adapts to what you are looking for.

Music can adapt to the narrative and pacing while adding atmosphere and impact to your project. If you want that, you need original music.

What my work focuses on

  • Original music for games, film and audiovisual media
  • Music adapted to your project from the first moment
  • Use of professional tools for film and video game music
  • Production, editing, mixing and project finishing
  • Atmosphere, pacing and emotional framing
